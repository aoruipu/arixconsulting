Who is responsible

ARIX Consulting AB is intended to be the controller for personal information submitted through this website. Contact: aoruipu@outlook.com.

Information collected

The contact form asks for your name, company, work email, company size, service interest, hiring challenge, preferred timeline, and consent to be contacted. You choose what detail to provide.

Why it is used

Information is used to understand and respond to your enquiry, discuss potential services, and maintain a limited record of the conversation. It is not used for automated decision-making.

Form processing

The production contact form is designed to use Formspree as a form-processing provider. Its processing terms and data location should be reviewed when the final account and endpoint are configured.

Cookies and analytics

The launch version does not use behavioural analytics, advertising trackers, or nonessential cookies.

Retention and rights

Enquiry information should be retained only for an approved business and legal period. Depending on applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or objection. Send requests to the contact address above.

Updates

This notice should be updated when the legal entity details, service providers, data practices, or applicable requirements change.
