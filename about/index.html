About ARIX

Recruitment judgement strengthened by data.

ARIX was created to close the gap between filling today's roles and preparing intelligently for tomorrow's hard-to-fill hiring.

Founder

AoRui Pu

Recruitment, sourcing, talent intelligence, and people analytics practitioner based in Sweden.

Email AoRui LinkedIn

AoRui brings nearly 10 years of experience across full-cycle recruitment, technical sourcing, talent partnership, HR support, analytics, and HR-tech initiatives.

His career includes recruitment and people roles with Inter IKEA Group, Truecaller, eBay, and Apple. The experience spans technical and digital hiring, proactive sourcing, stakeholder partnership, process development, market intelligence, and cross-functional project work.

Recent work has focused on applying analytics and AI responsibly to recruitment: making patterns visible, creating explainable signals, and helping teams decide where earlier sourcing or a different hiring strategy may be needed.

ARIX turns that experience into practical client work. It does not treat a dashboard as the answer by itself. The value comes from connecting evidence to recruitment action and keeping professional judgement in the loop.
